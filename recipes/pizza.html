Pizza Recipe

Description

A delicious homemade pizza with a crispy crust and a variety of toppings, including tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, pepperoni, mushrooms, and bell peppers.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup diced bell peppers

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to the desired shape and thickness.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a 1-inch border around the edges.
  4. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the tomato sauce.
  5. Arrange the pepperoni, mushrooms, and bell peppers on top of the cheese.
  6. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
